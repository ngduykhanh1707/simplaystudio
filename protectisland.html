Protect Island

Just tap to play a retro game!. King Kong, the king of the island is fighting against enemies.

A wonderful island which locates outside the world. There are many big animals are living good. Bad guys comes and fire rockets to destroy all of them.

King Kong, the king of this island is fighting against these enemies. All you need to do is help him defense coming missiles.


HOW TO PLAY

Tap on left or right side to throw coconut tree to defense the rockets. Remember that you should not not miss any of rockets otherwise the island will be destroyed.
