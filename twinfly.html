Twin Fly

Two boys who are practicing to fly. But at the same time only one can fly.

How to Play

Use a finger (just ONE finger) to touch on the screen and hold until a man flies up then release and touch the screen again for the other man flies up too before he hit the floor.

Don't hit the floor, don't hit the roof and watch out the flycam which can shoot a laser to kill these men.

Don't quit, stay on the flight and don't forget to share your score with friends.
