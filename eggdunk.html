Egg Dunk 3D

Lost eggs, they’re making a plan for finding the way back home on their high nests but seem it’s a very hard work. Will you help them?

How to Play

Drag to aim and release to shoot.


Game features

* Easy control by one finger
* Arcade mode and level mode
* Many egg skins for fun
* Challenging by barrier objects such as wind, cannon, bow, fire
