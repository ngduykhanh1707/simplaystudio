Game modes:

1. Timer:

- You have 60s to shoot the goal to reach target of score. Every round will be harder.

- Bonus 30 seconds when you score with dunk three times continuously.

2. 3 Balls:

- You have 3 balls to shoot until you lose all balls whenever you miss a goal the ball will be destroyed.

- Bonus 1 ball when you score with dunk three times continuously.
